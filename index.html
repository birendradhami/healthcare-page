Apptunix Excel In Developing Compliance-Friendly Healthcare Apps

We understand the importance of adhering to healthcare regulations. Our dedicated team ensures that our healthcare mobile apps meet the highest standards for data security and regulatory compliance, providing peace of mind for both patients and healthcare providers.

  • GDPR

    General Data Protection Regulation

    GDPR imposes strict data protection regulations to safeguard patient privacy. Our telemedicine app development solutions align with GDPR compliance, implementing robust data protection measures, including data encryption, and user rights management.

  • SaMD

    Software as a Medical Device

    SaMD refers to software intended for medical purposes. Our medical app development focuses on designing and developing medical-grade applications that comply with international standards for safety, efficacy, and quality.

  • HITECH

    Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health

    Our doctor on-demand app development company aligns with HITECH requirements to ensure that electronic health records (EHR) and patient data are protected against breaches, unauthorized access, and data loss.

  • HL7

    Health Level Seven International

    HL7 sets global standards for the exchange, integration, and retrieval of electronic health information. In our medical app development company, we adhere to HL7 ensuring seamless interoperability among healthcare systems, facilitating the secure transmission of patient data.
